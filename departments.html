Our Academic Departments

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda offers industry-oriented diploma programs designed to equip students with practical knowledge, technical competence, and professional ethics.

Engineering Branches


Computer Engineering

Programming, networking, databases, cybersecurity, and modern software development technologies.

Mechanical Engineering

Manufacturing, thermal engineering, machine design, production systems, and workshop practice.

Electrical Engineering

Power systems, electrical machines, industrial automation, and electrical installations.

Civil Engineering

Structural design, surveying, construction technology, transportation engineering, and infrastructure development.

Electronics & Communication

Communication systems, embedded systems, signal processing, and circuit design.

Applied Sciences

Mathematics, Physics, and foundational sciences that support all engineering disciplines.
