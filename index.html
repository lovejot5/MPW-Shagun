Government Polytechnic College Bathinda

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda, established in 1985, is one of Punjab’s premier technical institutions committed to academic excellence and industry-oriented diploma education. With a strong foundation in engineering and applied sciences, the college focuses on practical learning, innovation, and skill development to prepare students for professional success.

Spread across a well-developed campus with modern laboratories, workshops, and experienced faculty, the institution promotes holistic development through academics, sports, cultural activities, and placements. Our mission is to nurture competent engineers, ethical leaders, and responsible citizens for the nation.

    Principal’s Message

    Dr. Anuja Pupneja Principal, GPC Bathinda

    Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in 1985 to impart practical and quality technical education in Engineering and Technology. The institute has been honored with the Outstanding Institution Award and the Best Polytechnic Award by the Government of Punjab.

    The dedication of the faculty and staff ensures continuous academic excellence and innovative problem-solving approaches. We strongly emphasize holistic personality development by encouraging students to excel in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities, enabling them to become competent professionals and responsible citizens.
