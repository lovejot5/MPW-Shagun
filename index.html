    Principal’s Message

    Dr. Anuja Pupneja
    Principal, GPC Bathinda

    Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in the year 1985 to impart practical and quality education along with sufficient job opportunities in the field of Engineering and Technology. The Institute has been awarded with the Outstanding Institution Award (2019-20) and (2013-14) and the Best Polytechnic Award (2008-09) by the Government of Punjab. The perseverance and dedication of the entire staff is so high that it is effervescent to evolve problem- solving strategies for the betterment of the students. Holistic development of the personality of each and every student is stressed upon by encouraging students to perform best of their abilities in academic and curricular activities. Our students are the proud achievers of not only the academic positions but the topmost ones in sports also at the Inter-College Level.
