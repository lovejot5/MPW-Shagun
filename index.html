Principal's Message

Since its establishment in 1985, Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda has remained committed to excellence in diploma education. Our mission is to nurture technically skilled professionals who demonstrate innovation, discipline, and ethical values.


We strive to provide modern infrastructure, strong placement assistance, and a disciplined academic environment for holistic development of our students.
