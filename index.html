Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda

Established in 1985, Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda stands as a pillar of technical education in Punjab. With a commitment to academic excellence, discipline, innovation, and industry alignment, the institution has continuously nurtured skilled diploma engineers who contribute meaningfully to society and the nation. The campus fosters a culture of integrity, professional competence, and practical exposure through well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong placement support.

    Principal's Message

    Ar. Anuja Pupneja

    Principal, Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda

    At Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda, our mission is to cultivate technically competent professionals who embody innovation, discipline, and ethical responsibility.

    Since its inception, the institution has upheld a tradition of academic rigor combined with practical exposure. We believe in holistic development — nurturing both technical proficiency and character — so that our students emerge as confident contributors to industry and society.

    With dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and strong industry collaboration, we continue to move forward with vision, integrity, and commitment to excellence.
