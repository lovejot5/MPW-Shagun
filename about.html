About Government Polytechnic College

Established in 1985, Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda stands as one of Punjab’s leading diploma-level technical institutions. The college is committed to delivering high-quality, industry-oriented education in Engineering and Applied Sciences while maintaining strong academic discipline and professional ethics.

With modern laboratories, well-equipped workshops, experienced faculty, and a student-centric environment, the institution continuously evolves to meet the changing demands of technology and industry. Our focus remains on practical learning, innovation, leadership development, and employability.

Institution Overview


Located on Bibiwala Road, approximately 3 km from Bathinda Bus Stand, the campus spreads across more than 22 acres of structured academic blocks, technical laboratories, workshops, library facilities, sports infrastructure, and green surroundings.


Over the decades, the college has earned recognition for academic excellence and has been honored by the Government of Punjab for its contribution to technical education. The institution emphasizes skill development, innovation, entrepreneurship, and holistic personality growth.

Vision & Mission


Our Vision

To become a premier diploma-level technical institution recognized for academic excellence, technological innovation, ethical leadership, and strong industry collaboration.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education through modern infrastructure, competent faculty, industry exposure, and continuous skill enhancement programs that prepare students for global opportunities.

Core Objectives


Technical Competence

Developing highly skilled diploma engineers equipped with practical and analytical abilities.

Entrepreneurial Growth

Encouraging innovation, startups, and self-employment initiatives among students.

Holistic Development

Promoting leadership, ethics, teamwork, sports, and co-curricular excellence.
