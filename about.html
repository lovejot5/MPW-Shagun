About Our Institution

Established in 1985, Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda has consistently delivered excellence in technical education.

College Overview

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in the year 1985 with the objective of imparting quality diploma education in Engineering and Technology. The institution serves students across southern Punjab and is known for its academic discipline, modern infrastructure, and industry-oriented curriculum.


Located on Bibiwala Road, approximately 3 km from Bathinda Bus Stand, the campus provides a peaceful academic environment combined with state-of-the-art facilities.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading institution in diploma technical education by producing competent professionals equipped with innovation, technical expertise, and ethical values.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education through modern laboratories, industry collaboration, and disciplined academic practices that prepare students for professional success.
