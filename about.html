About Our Institution

Established in 1985, Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda continues to deliver excellence in diploma technical education with innovation, discipline, and industry relevance.

Institution Overview

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in 1985 with the objective of providing high-quality diploma education in Engineering and Technology.


Located on Bibiwala Road, approximately 3 km from Bathinda Bus Stand, the campus spans over 22 acres of well-planned infrastructure including academic blocks, workshops, laboratories, sports facilities, and green landscapes.


The institution focuses on academic excellence, skill development, entrepreneurship, and holistic personality growth.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading diploma-level technical institution recognized for innovation, technical excellence, and ethical leadership.

Our Mission

To provide industry-oriented technical education through modern infrastructure, strong academic discipline, and continuous skill enhancement programs.

Core Objectives

Skilled Manpower

Preparing technically competent professionals for industry needs.

Entrepreneurship

Encouraging self-employment and startup initiatives among students.

Holistic Development

Promoting extracurricular activities and leadership development.
